FAQs

Can I repatriate my sale proceeds to my country of residence?

Yes, our comprehensive Money Repatriation services make this process seamless. Under RBI and FEMA regulations, NRIs and PIOs can repatriate up to USD 1 million annually from an NRO account to an NRE or overseas bank account. We assist with the necessary tax compliance, including Form 15CA and 15CB certifications, ensuring smooth, legal, and hassle-free cross-border transactions.

What are the 2026 Capital Gains tax rates for selling property in India?

When selling assets in India, tax implications depend on the purchase date.
For properties bought before July 23, 2024, NRIs can choose a 12.5% tax rate without indexation or 20% with indexation.
For purchases after that date, a flat 12.5% rate applies.
We provide expert guidance and a forensic tax audit before your property sale to minimize fees and maximize your returns.

How do you protect vacant land or apartments from encroachment?

Prevention is better than cure. Our Plot & Land Monitoring service offers a robust security solution:
Regular physical site visits by your local asset custodian.
Installation of prominent "Ownership & Warning" signage.
Installation of fencing or compound walls (if requested by the property owner)

Can you manage a property that is involved in a legal dispute?

While we do not provide direct legal counsel, we offer extensive support for properties in dispute. Our team can point you to capable property lawyers, coordinate with them regarding your case, gather on-ground evidence, and communicate with municipal bodies and law enforcement.

How do you ensure I am not being overcharged for property repairs?

Transparency and trust are key values of our property management services. We have a team of authorize vendors who we’ve been working with for a long time and you can be assured of the quality of their work. We guarantee good work for fair prices as well as detailed explanations of the repairs that are being undertaken and why.
